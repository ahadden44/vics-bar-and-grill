Welcome to Vic's

Your neighborhood bar for cold drinks, fresh food, and nights that feel like home.

Our Story

Vic's Bar & Grill is a true neighborhood place, built around local owners, friendly staff, and the kind of regulars who make the room feel familiar the moment you walk in.

From fresh-ground burgers to cold beer and live events, the goal has always been simple: make a spot people want to return to again and again.

Our Mission

  • Quality Food — Fresh ingredients, house-ground burgers, and a menu built for real appetite.
  • Cold Beer & Drink Specials — Full bar service, happy hour 10-6, and the kind of drinks that keep the night moving.
  • Neighborhood Fun — Trivia, karaoke, TouchTunes, and pull tabs in a room that stays lively without losing its neighborhood feel.

Our Community

Vic's is where local people meet for casual lunches, weekend dinners, trivia nights, and private celebrations. We value familiar faces, new guests, and the easy kind of hospitality that makes a bar feel like part of the block.
