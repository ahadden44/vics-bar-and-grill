Our Story

Vic's Bar & Grill isn't just another place to grab a bite. We're a typical corner bar with local owners, friendly staff, and a fun atmosphere where neighbors and visitors feel welcome.

Our hamburger is ground fresh daily with special spices that make it spectacular and worth the drive.

What Drives Us

  • Quality Food — Every dish starts with fresh ingredients and care.
  • Cold Beer & Drink Specials — Full bar, happy hour 10-6 with $2.50 rails and bottles.
  • Live Music & Fun — Live music, trivia, TouchTunes, and a lively crowd.

Your Neighborhood Gathering Spot

Whether it's a casual lunch, weekend dinner with friends, trivia night, or a special event, Vic's is where you belong. We take pride in knowing your name, remembering your usual order, and making sure you feel at home.
